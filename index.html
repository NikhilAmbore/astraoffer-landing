How it works

Zero setup. Instant answers.

Join your interview call as normal. AstraOffer runs silently in the background, invisible to everyone else.

01
Captures interviewer audio
Listens to your speakers via system audio loopback — the interviewer's voice, not yours. No microphone. No recording. Works on Zoom, Teams, Meet, and any video call.
02
Transcribes & detects questions
Groq Whisper transcribes speech in real-time with 5-second audio chunks for maximum accuracy. Smart question detection fires the moment the interviewer finishes speaking.
03
Streams a perfect answer
Claude Sonnet generates a STAR-structured answer grounded in your actual resume — company names, dates, metrics, projects. Ready to speak in under a second.

Interviewer question
"Design a notification service that can handle 10 million users."
AstraOffer answer
I'd start with requirements: push, email, SMS. At 10M users, fan-out is the core problem. I'd use Kafka with per-channel consumer groups. Notification preferences sit in Redis for sub-millisecond reads. Email/SMS delegated to SendGrid/Twilio via async workers. Rate-limit per user at the queue layer. Delivery receipts in Cassandra. At 10M that's ~50K events/sec at peak — fully horizontal.

After download

Setup guide — 5 minutes.

Follow these steps exactly. Takes one time only.

1
Install the app
Mac: Double-click the .dmg, drag "AstraOffer Assistant" to Applications, eject the DMG.
Windows: Run the .exe installer, follow the prompts, launch from the Start menu or Desktop shortcut.
2
First launch — bypass the Mac security warning
Mac — if you see "damaged and can't be opened":
Open Terminal (⌘ Space → Terminal) and run:
xattr -cr "/Applications/AstraOffer Assistant.app"
Then launch the app normally. ⚠ One-time only — the app opens instantly from then on.

Windows — if you see "Windows protected your PC":
Click More info → Run anyway. Safe to proceed.
3
Grant Screen Recording permission (Mac only)
System Settings → Privacy & Security → Screen Recording → enable AstraOffer Assistant. Restart the app after granting.
4
Sign in with your AstraOffer account
Open the app → enter your email → Continue. If you don't have an account yet, create one at astraoffer.org in 30 seconds. No credit card needed. AI is included — no extra keys required.
5
Upload your resume and start your session
In the app → Setup → paste your resume and job description → Start Session. During interviews: Cmd+Shift+S (Mac) / Ctrl+Shift+S (Windows) to listen, or Cmd+Shift+C / Ctrl+Shift+C to capture a coding problem.
